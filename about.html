About EEES

Empowering the next generation of electrical and electronic engineers

Our Story

The Electrical & Electronic Engineering Society (EEES) was founded in 2018 with a vision to create a platform where students could explore, innovate, and excel in the field of electrical and electronic engineering.

What started as a small group of passionate students has grown into one of the most active and influential societies at South Eastern University of Sri Lanka, fostering innovation, collaboration, and professional development.

Our Mission

To empower students with technical excellence, innovation, and leadership skills while fostering a collaborative learning environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading engineering society that shapes future technology leaders and innovators in Sri Lanka and beyond.
