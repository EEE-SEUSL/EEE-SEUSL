About EEES

The Electrical & Electronic Engineering Society (EEES) of South Eastern University of Sri Lanka is a dynamic student organization dedicated to fostering innovation, learning, and professional development in the field of electrical and electronic engineering.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, providing our members with opportunities to engage in cutting-edge research, industry collaborations, and skill development programs.

Our Mission

To empower students with technical excellence, innovation, and leadership skills in electrical and electronic engineering.

Our Vision

To be the leading engineering society that shapes future technology leaders and innovators in Sri Lanka.
